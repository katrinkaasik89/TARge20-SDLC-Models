Waterfall: Advantages and Disadvantages

Advantages Disadvantages
⚡ Waterfall relies on teams following a sequence of steps and never moving forward until the previous phase has been completed. ⛔ Excludes the client and/or end user.
⚡ Determines the end goal early. ⛔ Delays testing until after completion.
⚡ Transfers information well. ⛔ Makes changes difficult.
