Scrum: Advantages and Disadvantages

Advantages Disadvantages
⚡ Scrum ensures effective use of time and money. ⛔ Adopting the Scrum framework in large teams is challenging.
⚡ Scrum, being agile, adopts feedback from customers and stakeholders. ⛔ The chances of project failure are high if individuals aren't very committed or cooperative.
⚡ Large projects are divided into easily manageable sprints. ⛔ Quality is hard to implement until the team goes through an aggressive testing process.
